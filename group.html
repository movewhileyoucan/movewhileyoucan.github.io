Group Training

Want to train with some friends? No problem. Save money and move within a small group.

Do you want a personal trainer but perhaps are nervous about one-on-one training? Then group training may be the right option for you. Training within a small group is a great way to get fit without the high cost, not to mention getting fit with your friends can be a lot of fun.

I will create a unique 45 minute program that will focus on strength training, muscular endurance and coordination.

Motivate yourself and your friends. If you're interested, gather some friends ( max size of group training is 4 people) and move together.

Special prices for regular weekly sessions can be arranged.
