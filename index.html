Why "move while you can" ?

Well, why the hell not? Our bodies were meant to move, run, jump, dance etc, so why are you just sitting there? I don't blame you, the problem is that today's life is very different from back in the days when were hunters and gatherers. Most of us now are sitting for over 8 hours a day, hunched back in our chairs and staring at screens. It's no wonder that we have back pains, are constantly fatigued and don't know how to activate certain muscles (When is the last time you felt your glute muscles?). Making even the smallest adjustment to your life can make all of the difference, for example elevators and escalators are all man made, try to avoid them when you can. Just move! I want to help you awaken your body and show you functional movements that will make your day to day life easier. We're all meant to be strong and active regardless of age, so move while you can.
