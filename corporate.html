Corporate Wellness

I believe that corporate health and fitness programs leads to a reduction of employee absenteeism. Employees who are stressed or unhealthy tend to become sick much more often than healthy employees and when employees are healthy and less stressed they tend to take less sick leave.

The food we consume can influence our daily performance, making us feel lethargic and wishing we lived in parts of the world where taking a siesta is a way of life. Food should give us energy and not make us feel tired.

Companies should be more proactive about encouraging a healthy lifestyle for employees, which can be difficult at times with so many unhealthy options available. Here is where I come in as your company's health and wellness consultant to help you feel full,energized and satisfied.

  • design weekly healthy lunch menu using restaurants from the 10bis options. (including kosher or vegan options)
  • snack ideas
  • individual employee guidance
  • happy hour/fun day ideas
  • health newsletters
  • weekly workout sessions
