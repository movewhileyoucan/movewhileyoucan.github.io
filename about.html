About Me

Up until 2011, I would say that my health was just average. I was in my early 20's and had back aches, I felt weak and tired, had no muscle mass whatsoever and I couldn't even do one body-weight push-up. I hated the idea of working out and my mind frame was if I want to lose weight, I can just "not eat". After years of yo-yo dieting, I just gave up, I figured it just wasn't in my genes to be fit. After my father passed away of a stroke in 2010, I was changed. While visiting my father in the hospital surrounded my sick people all around me, it was the nurse that said something that would change my life from that moment on: "Most of the people in here are sick because of the food they eat and lack of movement" and that was it, from that moment on something changed and would never be the same again. I started to eat better, I began moving my body and I've never looked back ...
