What should I expect?


After I get a better understanding of your lifestyle and training history, I will create a routine for you that is based on your goals and capabilities. The training will be outdoors and will last 45 minutes. Be prepared to be pushed and to work hard. I train no matter rain or shine.

Where does the training take place?


I can recommend some good and central locations but I can also work with what suits you best. We can work in the area of your home or office, we just need open space in an outdoor environment.

Housecalls:
Bad weather is a part of life, but it shouldn't stop us from working out. If you have enough space, it is possible to arrange the session in the comfort of your own home.

A towel and water bottle
