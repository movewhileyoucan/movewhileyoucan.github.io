High Intensity classes

Why?


Work on your cardio, strength, conditioning in a 30 minutes body-weight Workout. Short bursts of high intensity with recovery periods have proven to be the best way to increase your fitness and lose fat.

Who?


Men and women of all ages and fitness levels are welcome!

What do I need?


We'll be working on grass, so bring a towel or fitness mat if you'd like. You will sweat... so bring some water!

When and where?


Training times and locations will vary, so for the next meetings write me an e-mail or check out my Facebook events.

Why 5 Euros?


Because it's affordable, fast and there is no commitment! Come when you want and as many times you want.

Anything else?


If you're not sure if you are able to exercise due to a medical condition, find out by answering a few questions.
